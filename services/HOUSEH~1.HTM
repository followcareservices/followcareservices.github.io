What Is Household Assistance?

Household assistance supports people with disability to manage domestic tasks that are difficult or unsafe to do independently. A clean, well-organised home is essential for health, safety, and quality of life — and our support workers are here to help you maintain yours.

We don't just clean — we work alongside you at your pace, in a way that respects your home, your privacy, and your preferences. Whether you need weekly support or occasional help, we'll build a schedule that works for you.

Our workers are fully screened, trained, and experienced in providing quality domestic support across Adelaide metro and surrounding areas.

What We Help With

Every person is different. We tailor our support to your goals, preferences, and NDIS plan.

🧹

General Cleaning

Vacuuming, mopping, dusting, and surface cleaning throughout your home on a regular or one-off basis.

🍳

Meal Preparation

Shopping, cooking, and meal planning to ensure you have nutritious food that suits your tastes and dietary needs.

👕

Laundry & Ironing

Washing, drying, folding, ironing, and putting away clothing and household linen.

🚿

Bathroom & Kitchen Cleaning

Thorough cleaning of bathrooms, toilets, sinks, showers, and kitchen surfaces.

Rubbish Removal

Collection, sorting, and disposal of household rubbish and recyclables on a regular basis.

🧺

Tidying & Organisation

Decluttering, organising cupboards, and helping maintain a tidy and functional living space.

Support with shopping — in store, online, or click-and-collect — to keep your pantry stocked.

Minor Home Maintenance

Basic yard tidying, garden watering, and light indoor tasks to keep your home environment safe.

Who Is This Service For?

NDIS Participants

Household assistance is available to anyone with NDIS funding under the Assistance with Daily Life support category.

People with Physical Disability

If bending, lifting, or standing for long periods is difficult or unsafe, our support workers can take over these tasks.

People with Chronic Illness or Fatigue

Managing energy-draining household tasks can be a challenge — we can provide regular, reliable support so you can focus on your health.

Older Participants

Support for older Australians living with disability who want to remain living independently in their own homes.

Families and Carers

We can complement family support by covering domestic tasks, giving family members time to rest or focus on other needs.

Private Clients

Household assistance is also available without an NDIS plan — contact us for a quote.

NDIS Funding & Fees

Household assistance is funded under the Core Supports budget — specifically the Assistance with Daily Life support category in your NDIS plan.

All charges follow the NDIS Pricing Arrangements (Price Guide). We provide a clear schedule of support hours and rates before any service begins — no surprises.

We work with self-managed and plan-managed participants. Contact us if you are NDIA-managed to discuss your options.
