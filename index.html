    Who We Are

    Compassionate support that truly makes a difference

    FollowCare Services Pty Ltd provides tailored disability support services and assists NDIS participants to achieve their goals.

    We believe in promoting independence, dignity, and connection — every participant deserves personalised care that helps them thrive. Our team of qualified and dedicated support workers are committed to helping you reach your goals in a respectful, safe, and empowering environment.

    Our Mission

    To deliver person-centred support that enhances wellbeing and inclusion for all.

    Our Vision

    A community where everyone can live with dignity, independence, and purpose.

    Why Choose FollowCare?

    Qualified & Compassionate Team

    Our support workers are highly trained, police-checked, and genuinely care about making a positive impact in your life.

    Person-Centred Care

    We tailor every service to your unique goals, preferences, and needs. Your independence and dignity guide everything we do.

    Flexible & Reliable

    Dependable support services that adapt to your schedule and needs. We're here when you need us most.

    Local & Accessible

    Serving communities across Australia with flexible support options that fit your schedule and location.

    Explore Our Services

    Tailored Support for Your Independence

    Support services to assist NDIS participants with daily living, community participation and household tasks.

    Daily Living Support

    Assistance with personal care, meal preparation, cleaning, and everyday activities — always with respect and dignity.

    Community Participation

    Support to access social, recreational, or educational activities, building connection and confidence.

    Household Assistance

    Reliable help with domestic tasks to maintain a safe and comfortable home environment.

    Respite Care

    Flexible support for families and carers who need time to rest and recharge.
